Topic: Youtube in Safari
=============================

youtube stops songs after one song if we go to next tab.

solution:

first open the website youtube
Safari > Preferences > Security > selet all and click Plugin-in Settings

now we see adoble flash player for flash plugin acivate or not active
choose ON.

Note: we can make On for all the websites

Advanced ===> Choose Internet plug-ins Disable for Stop plugins to save power.

restart safari.
